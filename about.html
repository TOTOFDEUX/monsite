Self summary

Louis Labesse

I am a 4th year French-Spanish student at ENSAM, Engineering School of "Arts et Métiers" located in Paris, France. I am passionate of robotics, mechanics and electronics. I speak French, Spanish, English and German

During free time i enjoy doing sports: rugby, running, climbing and badminton.

My interests are:

• 3D softwares: modeling, simulations and 3D printing,

• Hardware: PC maintenance, Electronics fixing and component troubleshooting

• Open access blacksmithing: Handcrafting practical metal items for everyday needs.

• Meditation: Daily, following sessions on Medito App


Engineering, for me, begins with curiosity and ends in impact. I am constantly looking at the world through the lens of “what if?” Watching children play on a slide, I once wondered: what if we could harness that motion for energy? That moment led to a prototype for a game that turns physical activity into electricity. It was simple, but it symbolized something greater: my drive to connect observation, creativity, and engineering for real-world solutions. This mindset naturally brought me to robotics — a field where systems must think, adapt, and perform reliably.
